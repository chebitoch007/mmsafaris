About M&M Africa Safaris

Bringing you closer to Africa's wild heart !!

Our Story

For over a decade, M&M Africa Safaris has been creating unforgettable wildlife experiences across the African continent.

Founded by passionate conservationists and safari experts, we began with a simple mission: to share the breathtaking beauty of Africa's wildlife and landscapes while promoting sustainable tourism and wildlife conservation.

Today, we're proud to be one of East Africa's most trusted safari operators, having guided thousands of travelers through remarkable adventures while maintaining our commitment to environmental stewardship and community development.

Our Conservation Efforts

Tourism with purpose

  • Supporting anti-poaching initiatives
  • Funding wildlife corridors and habitat restoration
  • Education programs for local schools
  • Employment opportunities for local communities
